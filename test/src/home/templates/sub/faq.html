    FAQ

How does voterable work?

Voterable is a system that consolidates tips for business. Users who contribute to the this platform can earn points by getting votes for their contribution. Those points they earned can be exchanged for free access. We are currently working on a reward system that rewards contributing members with a monetary incentive.

How are reputation points calculated?

Your reputation points are calculated based on - (Number of times your tips are upvoted) minus (Number of times your tips are downvoted) plus (Number of times your comments/reviews are upvoted multiplied by 0.2).

What can I do if I feel that my copyrighted work has been infringed?

If you have any concerns regarding copyright infringement please notify us at this link. We will do the necessary investigations to confirm that you are the rightful and original owner of the information in the post. When you are verified, you have an option to either a)Removing the post that is in breach of the copyright or b)Take over as the owner of the post. As the owner of the original post, you will be eligible for the reputation points acquired by the post, which allows you free access and/or monetary rewards in the future.

Is the data collected protected and how is it used?

Voterable is in compliance with GDPR rules. Any data we collect are your about your usage are used for recommendations and rewards, they are strictly kept confidential and strictly for internal usage and not shared with any third parties. The data we collect are also kept in a safe and secure location.

What is voterable for?

Voterable consolidates a snapshots of tips, tricks and hacks for businesses. It serves as a reference for people who are looking for new ways to improve their business performance and designed for to help the community share solutions or identify easier ways to achieve their goals in business.
